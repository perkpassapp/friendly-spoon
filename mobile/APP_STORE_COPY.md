## Full Description

PerkPass brings local member deals into one simple mobile experience for active members.

Browse participating Philadelphia spots, save the places you want to revisit, and redeem offers with short time-sensitive codes at checkout. Your account, recent activity, and billing access stay in one place so using your membership feels fast and easy.

What you can do in PerkPass:

- Browse live local deals
- Filter by day and category
- Save favorite businesses
- Redeem offers with short codes
- View recent redemption history
- Access billing and support from your account

Current launch model:

- PerkPass mobile is designed for existing active members
- Membership purchase messaging stays out of the app for the first App Store release

Suggested reviewer note:

The app experience is focused on using an existing membership rather than selling digital content inside the app. Account deletion is available in-app from the Account screen.
